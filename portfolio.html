1.1.9 Project: Build a Soda

For my first project, I created a program for a game along with my group that would allow users to choose a soda through user input. The user could choose sodas like Sprite, Dr.Pepper, and Coke, and once the user types in what soda they want, a cup will be drawn under the correct soda. Then, a stream of soda will be drawn, and the cup will be filled up with the soda.
